## Problem Statement

Users earn points through challenges and medals but have no visibility into:
- ❌ Total points calculation (challenge + medals + bonuses)
- ❌ Current ranking among all users
- ❌ Position in community (where they rank)
- ❌ Progress toward next rank tier
- ❌ Leaderboard display (top performers)

Currently: Points are calculated internally but not displayed prominently or used for ranking.
