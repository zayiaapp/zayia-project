## Problem Statement

Users can't see their challenge progress:
- ❌ No view of completed vs available challenges
- ❌ Can't filter by category or difficulty
- ❌ No progress tracking (X of Y completed)
- ❌ No difficulty selector (easy vs hard)

**This story creates a challenges tab showing user progress with filtering and difficulty selection.**
