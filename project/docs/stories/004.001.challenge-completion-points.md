**Depends On:** STORY-003.003

## Problem Statement

Users complete daily challenges but have no way to upload proof photos and receive points:
- ❌ No photo upload for challenge proof
- ❌ No photo compression (large files)
- ❌ No Supabase Storage integration
- ❌ No points awarded for proof submission
- ❌ No validation of proof quality

Currently: Challenges exist but users can't complete them with proof.
